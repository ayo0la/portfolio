What's the core feel you want to match from Bruno Simon?

Bruno Simon's site has several distinct qualities. Which matter most to you?

A

Tactile, physics-driven interaction

Everything in the world reacts to your car — you bump into objects, knock things over, push tiles around. The world feels alive and satisfying to explore purely through collision.

B

Discoverable content through exploration

Projects, skills, and info are placed as objects scattered around the world — no menus. You literally drive to something to read about it. Navigation is movement.

C

Minimal UI — the world IS the interface

No HUD clutter, no zone prompts, no dialogue modals with buttons. Info appears as 3D signs and billboards in the world itself. You read things by driving near them.

D

Whimsy and playfulness as brand identity

The car is small and cute, objects bounce and wobble, sounds are satisfying. The whole experience communicates "this person builds things that are fun to use."
