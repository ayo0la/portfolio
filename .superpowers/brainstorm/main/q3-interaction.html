What kind of "alive" do you want?

A

Terminal navigation

You type commands to move through the portfolio — whoami, ls projects/, open mdfld. Content appears in response. Feels like a real CLI.

B

Scroll-driven cinematic

Deep scroll animations — sections snap in, text splits, images wipe. Like a film credit sequence. No game, just beautifully choreographed scrolling.

C

Cursor-reactive

The page responds to where your mouse is — magnetic elements, distortion effects, particles that follow the cursor. The whole thing feels aware of you.

D

Minimal 3D — objects, not a world

A rotating sneaker, an orbiting logo, a few 3D objects embedded in an otherwise flat page. Three.js as a detail, not the whole experience.

E

Hybrid — terminal shell + rich sections

Starts as a terminal. Commands unlock full-screen visual sections. Dark, typographic landing → command → boom, cinematic section expands. Best of both.
